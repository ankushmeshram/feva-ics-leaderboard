ICS Adversarial Robustness Benchmark

Comprehensive Evaluation of ML-based IDS Robustness under Adversarial Attacks

Benchmark Description

This benchmark evaluates machine learning-based intrusion detection systems against multiple adversarial attacks in Industrial Control Systems.

  • Datasets: SWaT, WADI
  • Threat Models: FGSM, PGD, CorrShift
  • Metrics: Robust Accuracy, Precision, Recall, ASR
